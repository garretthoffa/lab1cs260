The great Rock Paper Scissors game

This is a Rock Paper Scissors game that you can play against a computer.

Game Instructions:

In this game you will be chosing either Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer will pick one as well, and it will compare the two choices and determine a winner

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper
